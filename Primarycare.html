Internal Medicine Clinics:

Geriatrics Clinics

ABOUT Fortune Rise Health Care


Fortune Rise Hospital is a leading autonomous healthcare hospital with a distinguished 22-year legacy. It distinguishes itself through its innovative 'Practicing Medicine' approach, providing medical education that equips students to become hands-on healthcare professionals. Fortune Rise Hospital incorporates best practices derived from partnerships with renowned global healthcare institutions, offering a distinctive learning environment for students. Through initiatives such as patient-centered care (PCC), immersive learning experiences (ILE), and real-time clinical exposure (RCE), students at Fortune Rise Hospital expand their medical knowledge beyond traditional classroom settings.
